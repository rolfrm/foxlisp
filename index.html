Infinite zoom painting application. Currently supports all the colors. Click anywhere to paint. Use shift+Up/Down to zoom. Use middle-click+drag or arrows to pan. Touch: Two fingers to drag, one to paint. Click Q to show palette. A/Z to zoom, S/X change brush res. D/C change brush size. W to toggle front/back colors. For a demo try this.
